Frequently Asked Questions

NFC pet tags are small, durable tags embedded with NFC technology. When scanned with an NFC-enabled device like a smartphone, they provide instant access to your pet's information, such as their name, medical history, and your contact details.

When an NFC-enabled device comes close to the pet tag, the tag communicates with the device to display the stored information. You can customize the data on the tag, making it easy for someone who finds your pet to contact you or access important details.

No, most NFC tags don’t require a specific app. Simply tap the tag with any NFC-enabled smartphone to view the information. However, our advanced NFC pet tags are integrated with our web app, which offers additional features such as detailed pet profiles, medical history tracking, care reminders, and real-time updates.

Unlike traditional tags that only display static information, NFC pet tags provide dynamic and customizable data. They can also store more details, such as medical records, and are more resistant to wear and tear.
