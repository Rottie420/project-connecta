Product Overview

Smart NFC wearables like wristbands provide seamless access to events, gyms, and more with just a tap!

  • ✅ Convenient, hands-free access
  • ✅ Waterproof and durable design
  • ✅ Customizable for different purposes
  • ✅ Easy integration with ticketing and membership systems

Specifications

  • Chip: NTAG215
  • Memory: 504 bytes
  • Range: 1-2 cm (0.4-0.8 inches)
  • Dimensions: Customizable upon request
  • Waterproof and durable for daily wear

Smart NFC Wearables Use Cases

Explore the versatility of NFC wristbands in real-world applications.

Gym Access

Use your NFC wristband to gain easy, secure access to gyms, fitness centers, and workout areas.

Event Ticketing

Easily attend concerts, conferences, and sports events by tapping your NFC wristband for quick access.

Access Control

Use NFC wristbands as secure, touchless access keys for offices, private areas, and restricted zones.

IoT Trigger

Activate IoT devices or smart systems by tapping your NFC wristband. Trigger actions like unlocking smart locks or adjusting thermostats.
